About Company

TGLIA has been founded with the vision to bring the pure and nutritional value back into our foods. We are passionate about health, hygiene, safety and quality of the foods we all consume. Our vision is to provide foods that meet the above criteria at an affordable cost. We combined this vision with stellar customer service, so people get access to healthy foods in the most convenient way possible.

Logistics

We have a qualified logistics team that is well equipped to serve customers in India and internationally at a high level. We are able to handle domestic and international shipments. Our logistics team is trained in handling the logistics for the complete supply chain.

Our logistics team ensures

  • Products are packaged properly in quality packaging, stored and delivered as per schedule to ensure good shelf life, maintain the integrity and freshness of the products thus maintaining quality.
  • Proper Labeling is applied as per buyer and destination country requirements.

Sourcing

We have qualified sourcing teams that ensure products are sourced with highest quality as per buyer requirements. We keep in constant touch with markets all over the world to study the market needs and work with our suppliers and processors to ensure we can meet these needs continuously.

We ensure products we source meet the specifications and certifications provided by buyers. We maintain traceability of the raw materials. We keep in touch with the regulations of European Union and USA and work with our sourcing teams to ensure products that can meet these requirements are sourced. We only select suppliers and manufacturers who meet our high standards.
